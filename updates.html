Development & Training

Strengthen vocals and instruments, train section leaders, conduct workshops, improve arrangements, and begin regular songwriting and creative development sessions.

PHASE 3 • PLANNED03

Production & Releases

Develop original singles, worship covers, live sessions, music videos, stronger recording systems, and recurring worship experiences for young professionals.

PHASE 4 • LONG TERM04

Albums, Events & Wider Impact

Release an EP or album, hold worship events and performances, collaborate with other ministries, develop future leaders, and reach more young professionals through digital platforms.

PERMANENT GUARDRAIL05

Every phase must serve the YP vision.

Growth is not measured only by releases, views, events, or performances. Every project must strengthen the Young Professional Service, develop its people, or communicate its message through worship and music.

The platform must never become bigger than the purpose.
