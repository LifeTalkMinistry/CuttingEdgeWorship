WHO WE ARE

We are building worshippers, not just performers.

CuttingEdge Worship is a community of young professionals united by faith, music, and the desire to serve God with excellence and humility.

We create a space where experienced musicians, aspiring singers, creatives, and people who are still discovering their gifts can learn, contribute, and grow together.

OUR MISSION

To help young professionals belong to a healthy community, develop their God-given gifts, and use their time and talents for a purpose greater than themselves.
