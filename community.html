Develop people who can serve through worship.

We equip young professional worshippers, musicians, songwriters, and creatives to grow in faith, character, skill, commitment, and humility.

Develop • Create • Serve
SHORT-TERM VISION03

Become a dependable department.

Build a committed team, strengthen rehearsals, train developing members, and consistently support Young Professional Service programs.

Build the foundation.
LONG-TERM VISION04

Create music with wider impact.

Produce original songs, albums, live worship recordings, events, and performances that represent the voice and faith journey of young professionals.

Build the music. Multiply the impact.
SUCCESS05

People before platforms.

Success means serving the YP Service well, developing new leaders, helping beginners grow, and creating music that genuinely ministers to people.

The department grows when people grow.
